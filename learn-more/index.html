Leadership

2011 paved the way for my passion. As president of the students organisation Erasmus Student Network in Mannheim, I started to think about effective leadership. How can I empower our members that they enjoy contributing to the organisation? A lifelong journey begun.

Social Psychology

I became a trainer for the youth organisation ESN and helped new leaders to find into their role. Also I wrote my final thesis about fostering coaching leadership behavior at a plant in Hungary while studying Sociology and Business at University of Mannheim, and about how more women can get hired for team leader positions while studying International Business at Maastricht University. During my work at a consultancy firm focusing on the agile transformation, I realized that social psychology and the agile way of working have a lot in common. I decided that I wanted to learn how to bridge academia and practice.

Scrum Master

Therefore, I started an industrial PhD on leadership in agile teams at the Robert Bosch GmbH. And here I am today: As a Scrum Master at the Robert Bosch GmbH, I help teams grow. Still, I continue growing myself. Step by step I learn what it means to be a leader, and enable others to take on the lead.
